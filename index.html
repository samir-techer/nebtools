About Us

Built for Students, by Students

What is NebTools?

NebTools is a free educational platform designed specifically for NEB (National Examination Board) Grade 11 and 12 students in Nepal. We provide smart, automated tools that simplify complex calculations and save hours of manual work.

Our Mission

To democratize education by providing free, accessible, and powerful study tools that help every NEB student achieve academic excellence regardless of their background or resources.
